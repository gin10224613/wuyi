艺术表达区

Art Expression Zone

艺术表达模块旨在通过视觉设计、叙事建构与多媒体呈现， 探索传统武术文化在当代语境中的创新表达方式。 相较于单纯的知识传播，本模块更加注重“感性体验”与“文化感染力”， 通过IP形象塑造以及舞台艺术呈现， 将原本相对抽象的文化内容转化为可感知、可传播、可参与的艺术形式， 从而增强受众的情感共鸣与文化认同。

The art expression module explores innovative expressions of traditional martial arts culture in the contemporary context through visual design, narrative construction and multimedia presentation. It focuses more on perceptual experience and cultural appeal, transforming abstract culture into perceivable and communicable art forms through IP image building and stage art presentation.

非遗IP形象：「常武」与「极锦」

Intangible Cultural Heritage IP: "Chang Wu" & "Ji Jin"

以国家级非遗「五常十八般武艺」与健身气功「八段锦」为文化内核， 本项目打造「常武」与「极锦」双IP形象，通过年轻化、可视化的设计， 让传统武术与养生文化走进当代生活，实现文化的创新传播。

「常武」：非遗武艺的少年武者。以十八般武艺为魂，塑造Q版3D国风少年形象， 身着经典中式武服，眉心聚气纹，神态坚毅，展现刚劲有力、正气凛然的武者精神。 同时延展棍、剑、双节棍、长枪等兵器动作造型，并开发多情绪表情体系， 增强IP在多场景传播中的表现力。

「极锦」：养生功法的灵动少女。以武术与健身气功为灵感， 塑造具有东方美学的国风少女形象。双丸子头融合太极阴阳意象， 服装简洁利落，体现刚柔并济之美。通过多场景服装与情绪设计， 传递健康养生与文化传承的理念。

Based on the national intangible cultural heritage “Eighteen Martial Arts of Wuchang” and the traditional health exercise “Ba Duan Jin”, this project creates two core IP images: “Chang Wu” and “Ji Jin”. Through youthful and visualized design, traditional martial arts and wellness culture are brought into contemporary life.

"Chang Wu" represents a young martial artist rooted in traditional weapon arts. Designed as a Q-style 3D character, he embodies strength, discipline, and righteousness. His image extends into multiple weapon-based poses and expressive emotional variations, enhancing recognition and adaptability across media.

"Ji Jin" represents a lively girl inspired by martial arts and health exercises. Her design incorporates Tai Chi philosophy with soft visual elements and dynamic posture, symbolizing balance, vitality, and inner harmony. Multiple costumes and scenarios expand her cultural storytelling potential.

文创产品设计与延展

Cultural & Creative Product Design

围绕「常武」与「极锦」双IP形象，项目开发系列文创产品， 将传统文化转化为贴近日常生活的设计载体，实现文化的生活化表达。

「常武」系列涵盖数码周边（手机壳、耳机保护套、鼠标垫）、 家居用品（抱枕）、展示类周边（亚克力立牌、保温杯）以及文教用品（线圈本、卡套、手办）等， 强调力量感与武术精神的视觉呈现。

「极锦」系列则偏向生活与情绪表达，包含抱枕、马克杯、雨伞、眼罩、 帆布包与钥匙扣等产品，并结合竹林、书房、新春等场景进行视觉延展， 丰富IP应用空间。

整体设计秉持“守正不守旧”的理念，在保留传统文化精神的基础上， 以年轻化表达降低文化认知门槛，让非遗文化以更生动的方式融入当代生活。

Based on the dual IPs "Chang Wu" and "Ji Jin", a series of cultural and creative products are developed to transform traditional culture into everyday lifestyle items.

The "Chang Wu" series includes digital accessories (phone cases, earbuds cases, mouse pads), home products (pillows), display items (acrylic stands, tumblers), and stationery, highlighting strength and martial spirit.

The "Ji Jin" series focuses on lifestyle and emotional expression, including mugs, umbrellas, canvas bags, eye masks, and more, with extended scenarios such as bamboo forests, study rooms, and festive themes.

The design follows the concept of “preserving tradition while innovating,” combining cultural authenticity with modern aesthetics to make intangible heritage more accessible and engaging.

舞台艺术与影像表达

Stage Art & Visual Expression

将武术与舞台艺术相结合， 通过编排具有叙事性的舞台作品， 将动作、音乐与灯光进行综合设计， 形成具有视觉冲击力与情感张力的艺术表达形式。 此外，通过影像记录与短视频创作， 将舞台作品转化为可在线传播的内容， 进一步拓展其传播范围。 这一部分不仅是对传统武术的一种再创作， 也是对其审美价值与表现潜力的深入挖掘。

Integrate martial arts with stage art, and form an artistic form with visual impact and emotional tension by composing narrative stage works and integrating movements, music and lighting. In addition, through video recording and short video creation, stage works are transformed into online communicable content to further expand their communication scope.
