全球传播

本模块关注中国武术文化的国际传播。在全球化背景下，传统文化不再局限于国界， 而是参与跨文化对话和再诠释。通过构建多语言传播体系， 本项目旨在将武术文化转化为全球观众易于理解、参与和认同的形式。 除了动作技巧的传授，还强调哲学思想、历史背景以及礼仪文化， 使学习者能够全面理解武术的文化价值和精神内涵。

Global Communication

This module focuses on the international dissemination of Chinese martial arts culture. In the context of globalization, traditional culture is no longer confined within national boundaries, but participates in cross-cultural dialogue and reinterpretation. By constructing a multilingual communication system, this project aims to transform martial arts culture into a form that is understandable, engaging, and meaningful to global audiences. Beyond teaching physical techniques, it emphasizes philosophical thought, historical context, and ceremonial practices, allowing learners to fully appreciate the cultural value and spiritual essence of martial arts.

多语言教育资源

Multilingual Educational Resources

提供双语及多语学习资料，包括教学视频、图文电子书和文化指南。 这些资源旨在降低国际学习者的入门门槛，让他们不仅理解动作，还能深入了解背后的哲学和文化。 每个学习模块配有详细注释和案例分析，帮助学习者将理论与实践结合，增强学习效果。

We provide bilingual and multilingual learning materials, including instructional videos, illustrated e-books, and cultural guides. These resources are designed to lower the entry barrier for international learners, enabling them not only to understand movements, but also to explore the underlying philosophy and culture. Each module comes with detailed annotations and case studies to help learners integrate theory with practice and enhance learning outcomes.

跨文化传播策略

Cross-cultural Communication Strategy

本项目采用跨文化适配的方法，而非直接翻译内容。通过叙事、视觉媒介与现代叙事结合， 传统元素被重新诠释，以符合现代价值观和审美。同时，注重文化敏感性和多样性， 使武术在不同文化环境中都能被理解和接受，促进真正的国际文化交流。

Instead of directly translating content, this project adopts a cross-cultural adaptation approach, combining storytelling, visual media, and contemporary narratives to reinterpret traditional elements in ways that align with modern values and aesthetics. It also emphasizes cultural sensitivity and diversity, ensuring martial arts can be understood and accepted across different cultural contexts, promoting genuine international cultural exchange.

社交媒体与全球平台

Social Media and Global Platforms

项目积极利用YouTube、TikTok等国际平台进行推广，定期发布短视频、教程和创意内容， 与全球观众保持持续互动。同时，通过线上讨论和社区活动， 学习者可以分享心得、提出问题，形成开放的学习生态。

The project actively utilizes international platforms such as YouTube and TikTok, regularly publishing short videos, tutorials, and creative content to maintain continuous engagement with global audiences. Through online discussions and community events, learners can share insights, ask questions, and create an open learning ecosystem.
