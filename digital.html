数智体验场

数智体验模块致力于将数字技术与传统武术文化相结合， 探索沉浸式、互动式的文化体验方式。 在信息技术快速发展的背景下， 传统文化的传播方式正在发生深刻变化， 从单向展示转向多维互动。 本模块通过引入虚拟现实、全景展示与交互设计， 使用户能够以更加直观和参与性的方式接触武术文化， 从而提升体验深度与传播效果。

360°全景体验

通过嵌入360度全景展示， 用户可以在虚拟空间中自由观察武术动作与场景布局， 获得接近真实环境的沉浸式体验。 这一技术不仅增强了视觉表现力， 也为远程学习与展示提供了新的可能性。

互动式兵器展示

通过简单的交互设计， 用户可以点击不同兵器， 查看其历史背景与使用方式。 这一形式将静态知识转化为动态体验， 增强学习的趣味性与参与感。

Digital & Interactive Experience

The Digital & Interactive Experience module aims to combine digital technology with traditional Chinese martial arts culture, exploring immersive and interactive ways to experience culture. With rapid development of information technology, traditional culture dissemination is undergoing profound changes, shifting from one-way presentation to multi-dimensional interaction. This module introduces virtual reality, panoramic displays, and interactive design, enabling users to engage with martial arts culture in a more intuitive and participatory way, thus enhancing both the depth of experience and the effectiveness of dissemination.

360° Panoramic Experience

By embedding 360-degree panoramic displays, users can freely observe martial arts movements and scene layouts in a virtual space, achieving an immersive experience close to a real environment. This technology not only enhances visual impact, but also provides new possibilities for remote learning and exhibition.

Interactive Weapons Display

Through simple interactive design, users can click different weapons to view their historical background and usage. This approach transforms static knowledge into dynamic experience, enhancing learning interest and participation.
