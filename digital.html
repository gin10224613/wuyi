数智体验场

数智体验模块致力于将数字技术与传统武术文化相结合，探索沉浸式、互动式的文化体验方式。 在信息技术快速发展的背景下，传统文化的传播方式正在发生深刻变化，从单向展示转向多维互动。 本模块通过引入虚拟现实、全景展示与交互设计，使用户能够以更加直观和参与性的方式接触武术文化，从而提升体验深度与传播效果。

数字兵器旋转展示

使用交互式数字技术展示传统兵器的旋转与运动，用户可通过鼠标或触摸操作观察不同角度和细节。

Digital Experience

The Digital Experience module combines digital technology with traditional Chinese martial arts, exploring immersive and interactive cultural experiences. In the context of rapid technological development, traditional culture is moving from one-way display to multidimensional interaction. This module allows users to engage more intuitively and interactively with martial arts culture, enhancing the depth of experience and communication effect.

Interactive Weapon Rotation

Interactive digital display of traditional weapons allows users to rotate and view them from different angles.
