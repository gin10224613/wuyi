数智体验场

数智体验模块致力于将数字技术与传统武术文化相结合， 探索沉浸式、互动式的文化体验方式。 在信息技术快速发展的背景下， 传统文化的传播方式正在发生深刻变化， 从单向展示转向多维互动。 本模块通过引入虚拟现实、全景展示与交互设计， 使用户能够以更加直观和参与性的方式接触武术文化， 从而提升体验深度与传播效果。

The Digital & Interactive Experience module aims to combine digital technology with traditional Chinese martial arts culture, exploring immersive and interactive ways to experience culture. With rapid development of information technology, traditional culture dissemination is undergoing profound changes, shifting from one-way presentation to multi-dimensional interaction. This module introduces virtual reality, interactive design, and digital visualization, enabling users to engage with martial arts culture in a more intuitive and participatory way, thus enhancing both the depth of experience and the effectiveness of dissemination.

数字兵器旋转展示

用户可以通过左右按钮旋转数字兵器图片，观察不同角度和细节， 获得接近实物的沉浸式感受。

Users can rotate the digital weapon images using left and right buttons, observing different angles and details, gaining an immersive experience similar to a real object.

互动式兵器介绍

通过简单的交互设计， 用户可以点击不同兵器， 查看其历史背景与使用方式。 这一形式将静态知识转化为动态体验， 增强学习的趣味性与参与感。

Through simple interactive design, users can click different weapons to view their historical background and usage. This approach transforms static knowledge into dynamic experience, enhancing learning interest and participation.
