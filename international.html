全球传播

Global Communication

本模块关注中国武术文化的国际传播。在全球化语境下，传统文化不再局限于国界， 而是参与跨文化对话与再解读。通过构建多语言传播体系， 本项目旨在将武术文化转化为全球受众可理解、可参与、且有意义的形式。

This module focuses on the international dissemination of Chinese martial arts culture. In the context of globalization, traditional culture is no longer confined within national boundaries, but participates in cross-cultural dialogue and reinterpretation. By constructing a multilingual communication system, this project aims to transform martial arts culture into a form that is understandable, engaging, and meaningful to global audiences.

多语言教育资源

Multilingual Educational Resources

提供双语及多语学习资料，包括教学视频、图文电子书和文化指南， 帮助国际学习者理解动作及其背后的哲学文化意义。

We provide bilingual and multilingual learning materials, including instructional videos, illustrated e-books, and cultural guides to help international learners understand both the movements and the philosophical and cultural meanings behind them.

跨文化传播策略

Cross-cultural Communication Strategy

通过跨文化改编而非直接翻译，将武术文化以符合全球受众的方式重新演绎， 结合故事讲述、视觉媒体和现代叙事，增强文化可理解性和相关性。

Instead of directly translating content, this project adopts a cross-cultural adaptation approach, reframing martial arts culture in ways that resonate with global audiences. By combining storytelling, visual media, and contemporary narratives, traditional elements are reinterpreted.

社交媒体与全球平台

Social Media and Global Platforms

积极利用国际平台如YouTube和TikTok发布短视频、教程和创意内容，扩大全球影响力。

Actively utilize international platforms such as YouTube and TikTok to publish short videos, tutorials, and creative content, expanding global reach.
