Global Communication

This module focuses on the international dissemination of Chinese martial arts culture. In the context of globalization, traditional culture is no longer confined within national boundaries, but participates in cross-cultural dialogue and reinterpretation. By constructing a multilingual communication system, this project aims to transform martial arts culture into a form that is understandable, engaging, and meaningful to global audiences.

Multilingual Educational Resources

We provide bilingual and multilingual learning materials, including instructional videos, illustrated e-books, and cultural guides. These resources are designed to lower the entry barrier for international learners, allowing them to understand not only the physical movements, but also the philosophical and cultural meanings behind them. Through structured content design, learners from different cultural backgrounds can gradually build their knowledge system.

Cross-cultural Communication Strategy

Instead of directly translating content, this project adopts a cross-cultural adaptation approach, reframing martial arts culture in ways that resonate with global audiences. By combining storytelling, visual media, and contemporary narratives, traditional elements are reinterpreted to align with modern values and aesthetics. This strategy enhances both accessibility and cultural relevance.

Social Media and Global Platforms

To expand its global reach, the project actively utilizes international platforms such as YouTube and TikTok. Short-form videos, tutorials, and creative content are distributed regularly, creating continuous engagement with audiences worldwide. These platforms not only serve as channels for dissemination, but also as spaces for interaction and feedback, enabling a dynamic and evolving communication process.
